About Us

Lumière is a scented candle store offering a number of products catering to people from all walks of life. We believe that scented candles are not a luxury, but rather an important aspect of individuals welfare. We ought to create an ambiance of healthfulness and well-being by being a part of your daily routines and itineraries.

Candles

Our candles, infused with organic and carefully selected aromatic oils, are made with 100% natural soy bean wax for a long, clean burn. Every single item is handmade and hand-poured by skillful craftsman in our studio in Santry and will be a great addition to your daily regimen.


Our candles are available in small black jar with cork lid for €4 and exquisit, large, turqoise glass for €7.

Scents

Noir

Noir is infused with Lavender, Chamomile & Ylang Ylang Blend. The smell helps activate the brain in such a way that it helps slow the heart rate and can help muscles relax muscles to prepare for better slow-wave sleep.

Desir

Desir is infused with vanilla and musk. Vanilla is a natural aphrodisiac. Historically — from the Mayans to the French — the scent has been connected to sexual arousal while the scent of musk is innately sensual, and layers well with other fragrances.

Se Detendre

Se Detendre is infused with a mix of lemon, cedarwood, and bergamot. The aroma emitted by this blend can help relieve stress, alleviate anxiety, lift the mood and relax the body. Get the feeling of rejuvenation after lighting this one.

Concentrer

Concentrer is infused with peppermint, sweet orange, spearmint, and rosemary concoction. This mixture helps to calm the mind so it can focus, and invigorate it to get things done. Also, it boosts brain power for overall mental clarity and for a quick surge of energy.
